Who the F#@!k is Candice?

Hailing from half way across the world in Jakarta, Candice spent a great deal of her childhood moving from one location to another. In Singapore, she was introduced to a melting pot of different cultures and cuisines, and thus began her life-long love affair with food (especially Indian and French).

Since relocating to Los Angeles, Candice has been pursuing print and digital journalism. She currently works as the arts editor for Neon Tommy, a digital news outlet at USC. When she isn't consuming all her time with eating and barely making deadlines, Candice, a news junkie is probably listening to public radio, getting into accidents (she got on a bike for the first time last year) or watching an episode of Twin Peaks (or anything by David Lynch).
