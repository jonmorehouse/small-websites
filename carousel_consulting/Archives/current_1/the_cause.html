THE CAUSE

Altruism and participation in local outreach programs are the real passions of Carousel Consulting. Nothing matters more than our relationship to each other and to the planet we share. There are several areas in which Carousel takes a particular interest.

Music and art, which allow us to express the profound nature of the human experience unencumbered by language or background; sports, which introduce health, discipline and teamwork into faltering communities; environmental conservation, offering a chance to clean up the mess humanity has made, and education for underprivileged youth, empowering them to promote positive change in their neighborhoods and beyond. It is our responsibility, and also our privilege, to enhance the well-being of our global community by contributing to a wide array of organizations.
